The Paintless Dent Repair Process has a few steps. Here is our full process for fixing your vehicle:







1 - Filing a Claim

Filing an insurance claim for your hail damage? We can help with that!

Filing an insurance claim is the first step of our process. After a claim has been filed, your insurance company will contact our shop to schedule a damage estimation.


2 - Scheduling an Estimation

Once a claim has been filed with your insurance company, an insurance adjuster will be scheduled to visit our shop and inspects the damage on your vehicle.

While the adjuster is inspecting the damage on your vehicle, our staff of estimators accompany the adjuster to determine the most accurate damage valuation.

After the damage valuation has been determined, our technicians begin working on your vehicle.


3 - Ordering Parts

Given how severed the hail damage is, some of the parts on your vehicle may need to be replaced instead of being repaired.

When this occurs, our shop assumes responsibility for ordering the parts your vehicle needs. Our team of experts will find the best deals at competitive prices.
